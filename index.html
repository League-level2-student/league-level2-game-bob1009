Hobie's Awesome Dino Game

by: Hobie Schutt

This game was made for my level 2 project at The League of Amazing Programmers.
Click here to download and play!

1. Why I Made This Game

Because it's a simple design, but also fun and challenging, I did this because I didn't want a super simple game, but not a super complicated either.

2. What I Learned

That it was fun, but there was some of them are impossible to jump over without dying, but I fixed it and it was all good.

3. Architecture of My Game

You play as a dinosaur that has tiny legs and you have to jump over the evil cacti how will kill you if you hit them. The dino interacts with cacti who has to jump over them. All the classes interact with each other in different ways.

4. Technical Implementation

The technical implementation is super simple. I'm super proud of how I made dino look like it was actually jumping rather than teleporting.

5. Most Challenging Part

Collision and getting the cacti to actually spawn and move was pretty complicated, but I beat them with logic and asking my teacher.

6. Future Fixes and Updates

I would make it so there are also birds that fly over you so you can't jump into them, and have a changing background.
